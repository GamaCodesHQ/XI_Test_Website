Our Foundation

Who We Are

Xoma Investment (Pty) Ltd is a South African engineering and industrial supply company specialising in the distribution of mechanical and electrical components, conveyor maintenance, and steel fabrication.

We serve clients in the mining, energy, and industrial sectors — from supplying critical pump spares and electrical components to executing conveyor maintenance contracts and fabricating steel structures.

We deliver reliable solutions through innovation, quality workmanship, and sustainable partnerships — striving to be more than just a supplier, but a dedicated partner in your operational success.

Our proven track record includes successful supply to State-Owned Companies (SOCs) such as Eskom, demonstrating our capability to meet the stringent procurement, quality, and compliance requirements of South Africa's most critical institutions.
