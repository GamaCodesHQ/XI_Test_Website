Who We Are

Your Dedicated Industrial Partner

Xoma Investment (Pty) Ltd is a South African supplier and engineering company specialising in the supply, distribution, and maintenance of critical industrial goods and services.

From conveyor belt components and pump spares to valves, actuators, and electrical components — we keep your operations running. Founded on principles of quality and reliability, we serve mining, energy, and related heavy industries.
